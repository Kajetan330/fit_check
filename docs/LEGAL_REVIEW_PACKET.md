## Specific Questions For Counsel

- Whether the escrow-style language should be replaced with Stripe/payment-hold language.
- Whether creator payouts and refund windows comply with applicable marketplace/payment laws.
- Whether customer body/closet photo uploads need additional consent language.
- Whether minors should be explicitly prohibited at launch.
- Whether the platform needs a DMCA/copyright takedown workflow for creator content.
- Whether the creator/customer terms correctly classify creators as independent providers.
- Whether affiliate disclosure language is sufficient for Capsule Build recommendations.
- Whether dispute-resolution and arbitration clauses are appropriate.

## Launch Position Until Reviewed

- Do not market funds as formal legal escrow unless counsel approves.
- Use "payment held until delivery approval" in product copy.
- Keep all customer booking uploads private.
- Require explicit consent before publishing customer transformations.
